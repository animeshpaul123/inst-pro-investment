Guru Prasad Pai - Managing Partner - India

Services:  Fund Raising, Mergers & Acquisitions, New Markets, Board Advisory.

“Guru”, to colleagues &”Prasad” to friends as he is addressed, is Post Graduate from Mumbai University with 27 years of accounting, finance, credit control, marketing, cross border acquisition & integration experience, advertising & production. His professional colleagues who also happen to be his close friends call him “Jack of all seasons”.

Currently based out of Mumbai, Guru is the managing partner of Instaura Consulting (India operations). He has worked with the best & reputed global MNC’s in India & abroad like FDC Ltd, Unichem Labs, Pacific Internet, Al Ahlia Gulfline (Bottlers of coca cola) Network Associates, EBS worldwide, Thoughtshop Advertising & Production, etc. He was part of the management team that acquired two companies in the USA, (New York & New Jersey) & one in India and successfully managed the finance portfolio from 3 different zones at unearthly hours.

A workaholic to the core, his penchant for systems, processes, policies & financial numbers yielded him 5 successive promotions in 6 years at a very early stage in his career at FDC Ltd. His astuteness, honesty & sincerity is appreciated even by his erstwhile president from the USA company from him, he recovered close to USD$5 million by highlighting a fine print from the stock purchase agreement. While continuing to focus on finances, he also performed the role of an executive producer, he not only convinced his CEO to shoot abroad but also assisted in all areas of production, resulting in huge savings & enhanced profits.

He strongly believes that for any company to grow & be successful, there is just one secret. The company has to be “process driven & not person driven”. He has travelled extensively in India & abroad and finds the work culture abroad more conducive than in India.

The only vice with Guru on his personal side is, he may forgive the person who has tried to break his trust, but he never forgets.
